About Azure Bay Hotel

A boutique seaside hotel combining relaxed luxury, genuine warmth, and breathtaking views of the coast.

Since opening our doors, we’ve welcomed guests from around the world for holidays, honeymoons, and special celebrations by the sea.

What we believe

  • Every stay should feel personal and effortless.
  • Good design is calm, clear, and welcoming.
  • Local culture and community matter.

Our story

Azure Bay Hotel began as a small family project: a place where guests could unwind by the water without giving up comfort or good design. Over time, we’ve grown into a modern boutique hotel, but we’ve kept the same focus on warmth and attention to detail.

Today, our team blends hospitality, design, and local knowledge to create stays that feel both relaxed and memorable – whether you’re here for a single night or a full week by the sea.

Our values

  • Warm hospitality: Every guest is welcomed like an old friend.
  • Attention to detail: From room design to breakfast, the small things matter.
  • Sustainability: We reduce waste, support local suppliers, and care for our coastline.
  • Calm & clarity: Simple, clear design that’s easy to use and pleasant to look at.
