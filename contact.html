Where to Find us

Azure Bay Hotel is located on Seaside Road, a short walk from the beach and the town centre. Public transport and parking are available near the hotel.

By train:

15 minute walk from Azure City Station. Taxis and buses are available outside the station.

By car:

Follow signs for Azure Bay seafront. Limited on site parking is available for our guests.
